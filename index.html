Travel Guide

Airports to fly in

  • John Wayne (SNA): closest airport, about 15–20 min away from Disneyland, small and lovely, easy to navigate with short security lines
  • Long Beach Airport (LGB): small cute outdoorsy airport about 25 min away from Disneyland, easy to navigate
  • Los Angeles International (LAX): 45–60 min away in no traffic (up to 1.5 hrs in traffic), most flight options

Far away airports (would not recommend): Hollywood Burbank Airport, Van Nuys Airport, Ontario International Airport.

Getting around

Public transit is not great. Renting a car is usually the best option, otherwise Uber/Lyft works. Parking at the venue is limited, so rideshare/carpooling is highly encouraged.

FAQ

What’s the best way to get around?
Definitely by car. Public transit in Orange County certainly leaves something to be desired. Renting a car at the airport gives you the freedom to travel to all the fun spots around town, and is what we would recommend. Uber/Lyft is also an option! Parking spots at the venue are also limited, so rideshare/carpooling is highly encouraged.
Is this an indoor or outdoor wedding?
Hopefully outdoors if the weather allows! SoCal in May is usually dry, sunny, and ~70°F.
Can I hike in the park on the day of the wedding?
Yes! The park is open from 7am to sunset. We recommend bringing a change of shoes (and clothes) if you plan to do so. Most of the trails are not well suited for formal shoes. There are restrooms but NOT showers available in the park.
How early should I arrive to the venue?
You can enter the park as early as 7am, but if you don’t plan to hike before the wedding, we’d recommend arriving around (insert time) (e.g., 15 minutes before ceremony).

We don’t have one! Your presence is the greatest gift we could ask for, and gifts are not expected! If you would like to get us a gift anyway, consider something small and travel-friendly (such as a book or movie, a D&D accessory or miniature, a cat toy, a board game, a Steam key, etc.).

Nope! Please do not bring any non-human animals unless it’s a service animal. (Possible exception for cute cats — please text us photos to apply.)
